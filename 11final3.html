Help

​1. ​​Pro Tip: Keyboard banane waalon ne 'F' aur 'J' keys par ek chhoti si ubhri hui line (raised line) banayi hai. Iska maqsad ye hai ki bina keyboard ko dekhe, aapki index fingers aasani se wapas home row par apni jagah dhoondh sakein. ​3. Finger Responsibilities ​Keyboard ko is tarah baanta gaya hai ki har ek ungli ka apna ek fix area hota hai: ​Diagonal Movement: Har ungli apne se diagonally upar aur neeche waali keys ko control karti hai. (Jaise right hand ki middle finger 'K' se upar 'I' aur neeche ',' tak pahunchti hai). ​Pinky Fingers: Inka kaam thoda zyada hota hai, kyunki ye capital letters (Shift) aur paragraph badalne (Enter) jaise kaam bhi karti hain. ​Index Fingers: Ye do rows ki zimmedari sambhalti hain: ​Right Index: 'U', 'M', 'Y', 'H', aur 'N'. ​Left Index: 'R', 'V', 'T', 'G', aur 'B'. ​4. Typing Tips for Success ​Position your Hands: Apni ungliyon ko keyboard par thoda sa modkar (curve karke) rakhein taaki wo chalne ke liye tayar rahein. ​Don't Look Down: Keyboard ko dekhne ke bajaye use mahsoos karne ki koshish ​Note: Shuruat mein ungliyon ko stretch karna ajeeb lag sakta hai aur iske liye kaafi practice ki zaroorat hoti hai. Dheere-dheere aapki muscle memory develop ho jayegi.
